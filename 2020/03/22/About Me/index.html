Education

I did my undergraduate study in University of Science & Technology Beijing (USTB) and Beijing Information & Science Technology University(BISTU), majoring in Mechanical Engineering and minoring in Intelligent Robotic Technology. I was lucky enough to be selected as one of the first group of students for “Experimental Class” (实验班), an elite title accompanied by heigh-level training in mathematics and robotic. I finished the 1.5 year of intensified basic science training and chose to major in Robot Technology in the rest of my program. My thesis project was about mechanical structure design of rehabilitation Robot.

In 2018，I continued my study in professor Zhonglin WANG’s group in Beijing Institute of Nanoenergy and Nanosystems, Chinese Academy and Sciences, China as a master of science student. My research project is about Nanogenetor, Bio-inspired Intelligent Materials, MEMS and Flexible Electronics. It is intensive, even exhausting sometimes. I work with the best minds in this field and have access to the best instruments I can imagine. So, nothing to complain.

In future, I want to apply for PhD to do more valuable, interesting, challenging and interdisciplinary research about Wearable Electronics based on Nanogenerator to solve some medical problems in my PhD study.1-4
